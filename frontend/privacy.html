Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

What we do

Veris (veris.news) is a media bias analysis tool. You submit article URLs or text, and we analyze them using AI to surface political lean, sentiment, and factual reliability.

What data we collect

We collect only the content you explicitly submit — article URLs or pasted text. We do not collect your name, email, or any account information. We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.

How your data is used

Submitted content is sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. It is used solely to generate the analysis you requested and is not stored on our servers after the response is returned.

Anthropic may retain API inputs per their own privacy policy.

Data storage

We do not store, log, or retain any article content or analysis results. Each request is processed in memory and discarded immediately after the response is sent to your browser.

Third-party services

Anthropic — AI analysis engine. See anthropic.com/privacy.

Cloudflare — Content delivery and DDoS protection. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Railway — Backend hosting. See railway.app/legal/privacy.

Children's privacy

Veris is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the updated date above.
