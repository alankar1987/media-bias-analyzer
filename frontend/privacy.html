Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Veris ("we", "the service", "the extension") is a tool that analyzes news articles for political lean, tone, and factual accuracy. It is available as a website (veris.news) and as a Chrome browser extension. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you analyze an article:

  • The URL of the article you are analyzing
  • The article's text (fetched by our backend from the URL you submit, or text you paste in)
  • The timestamp of the request

If you sign in with Google (optional):

  • Your email address
  • A Supabase-issued authentication token

When you share an analysis:

  • When signed in, each analysis you run is saved to your account and assigned a unique unguessable ID. By default, anyone with the share link (for example, when you post it on LinkedIn or X) can view that analysis.
  • The public share view shows only the article URL, headline, source, scores, and analysis content. It never shows your name, email, or any other account information.
  • To disable sharing for an individual analysis or delete it entirely, email privacy@veris.news. (An in-product toggle is on the roadmap.)

If you subscribe to the Pro plan (optional):

  • Billing information processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details ourselves; Stripe handles payment data per their own privacy policy.

When you visit veris.news:

  • Aggregate usage data collected by Google Analytics, including page views, session duration, approximate location (country/city derived from IP), device type, and referring source. We use this only to understand how the site is being used in aggregate; we do not link it to your account.

What we do with it

  • Article URLs and text are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to perform the analysis. We do not share this data with any other third party.
  • Analysis results are cached by content hash for 30 days so repeat analyses of the same article are instant. The cache is shared across anonymous users.
  • If you are signed in, your analyses are saved to your account so they appear in "My History" on veris.news.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not analyze pages other than the one you explicitly click "Analyze" on.
  • We do not read your tabs, history, bookmarks, or any other browser data.

Data retention and deletion

  • Anonymous analyses are retained in the cache for 30 days, then deleted.
  • Signed-in users' analyses are retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing privacy@veris.news.

Third-party services

Anthropic — provides the AI model that performs analysis. Privacy policy

Supabase — handles authentication and data storage. Privacy policy

Stripe — processes payments for the Pro plan. Privacy policy

Google — provides Sign in with Google (only your email address is shared with us) and Google Analytics for aggregate site-usage statistics. Privacy policy

Railway — hosts the Veris backend. Privacy policy

Cloudflare — provides DNS and CDN for veris.news. Privacy policy

Children's privacy

Veris is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the updated date above.
